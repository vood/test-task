## Phase 1: Role & Daily Work

### Q: João hi, can you describe your role?

**A:** i am sales lead in brasil, report to maria silva. i work with the aes day to day on the bigger deals and i also carry my own book of larger accounts. so half manager half ae you can say. mostly são paulo but i travel for the bigger ones.

### Q: What's a typical day?

**A:** mornings i look at the pipeline, check what is moving and what is stuck. then customer calls. afternoons more calls and some coaching with the aes. friday is the pipeline review with maria.

## Phase 2: Knowledge & Expertise

### Q: What kind of deals do you personally own?

**A:** the bigger accounts, anything above two hundred k reais arr usually comes through me or through me supporting an ae. i have the relationship with most of these customers. some i've known since before helixpay acquired the brasil business. recently i closed a regional restaurant chain about forty locations, one hundred eighty k reais arr, signed in march. that one was nine months of work.

### Q: Tap product — how is it landing with your customers?

**A:** the form factor they love. no terminal, just the phone, very easy. but olha the reconciliation report is a problem. when a customer runs tap and loyalty together the report does not match what the merchant sees in their own till. so they call us and say your numbers are wrong and we have to go check and explain. some times it really is a bug in the integration, some times it is the merchant misreading the report but either way it is a bad conversation. i have lost two deals i think because of it. larissa also.

### Q: Anything else on the product side worth flagging?

**A:** the multi-property thing comes up here too sometimes for restaurant groups but it is not as common as i hear it is in sea. for us the bigger issue is the tap report.

## Phase 3: Processes & Tools

### Q: How is Pipedrive working for the team?

**A:** ah pipedrive. ok. eu vou ser honesto, o pipedrive tá uma bagunça. tem duplicatas em todo lugar, a história das contas tá quebrada em vários casos, e os dashboards, eu não confio neles. quando a maria me pergunta qual é a cobertura do pipeline eu tenho que ir conta por conta porque o número agregado tá errado. so to translate — the data is messy, lots of duplicates, account history is broken in many cases, and the dashboards i don't trust. when maria asks me what the pipeline coverage is i go account by account because the aggregate number is wrong.

### Q: Is the team excited about HubSpot?

**A:** mixed. we hear from sea that the training was not great. the team wants the change but they also are afraid of losing customer notes. i have customers i have notes on going back four years. if those notes do not come over clean i lose context i cannot replace.

### Q: Have you started any prep for the migration?

**A:** maria asked me and larissa to start cleaning duplicates in pipedrive in march. we have done maybe thirty percent of the cleanup. it is slow because for each duplicate we have to decide which record is the master and merge the notes manually.

## Phase 5: Problems & Ideas

### Q: What would help you most if you could wave a wand?

**A:** clean data. just clean data. one record per customer, one source of truth, the history actually right. that and fix the tap report.

### Q: Have you heard about any of the platform work happening in Singapore?

**A:** there is some platform thing in singapore, confluence i think it is called. i have heard the name. honestly i don't really know what it is. someone in a town hall said it was about unifying systems but i could not tell you what it means for me. nobody has explained how it affects sales in brasil.
